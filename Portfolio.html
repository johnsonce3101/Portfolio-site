Bio

Christopher "CJ" Johnson

An emanation of TIP, NIP, SWV, & Jodeci, Sí has a sound that has grown beyond the mountainous boundaries that served as an incubator to birth his sound. With influences from Rock, Jazz, West African Drumming Music, R&B and HipHop genre’s Si’s sound gives you a familiar feeling with excitement of hearing something. Born in on the south side of Atlanta in Riverdale, GA but raised in north east Tennessee in Knoxville gave Sí two very different perspectives of life which translates to his sound. The objective is to use music as Catalyst to inspire and create a collective conscious for others find purpose.
